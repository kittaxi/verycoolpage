I love this cat ...

... Or car, as I will refer to him sometimes. Here's some info about him:

The premise – The funny-looking car (seen above), is known as the "Canny cat", or its opposite, the "Uncanny cat" (try hovering or clicking the image!).

The expression combined with memes of the aforementioned creature make it the best meme of 2023 for me. Someone even made a ‘golf’ game about him!
