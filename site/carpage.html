The 'Uncanny cat'

I love this cat ...

The funny-looking cat (as seen above), is known as the "Canny cat", or its opposite, the "Uncanny cat" (try hovering on or tapping the image!).

I think the image is funny because the cat is looking at the camera with a certain expression, as if he accidentally opened the front camera on his phone.

The expression combined with memes of the aforementioned creature make it the best meme of 2023 for me. Someone even made a ‘golf’ game about him!

Facts:
– The cat's name is "Professor Jones"
– He's the cat of a Reddit user, /u/Lavonicus
– The cat has a Twitter profile here if you want to check out more images of the cat.
