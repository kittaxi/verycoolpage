I love this cat ... The cat above is known as the "Canny cat", or its opposite, the "Uncanny cat".

I think the image is funny, because his expression looks like he's absolutely done with something, as if he's thinking "this again.." or something similar :) I personally like to use it as an emoji in Discord for something that's mildly annoying.

The expression combined with funny edits of the image make it the best meme of 2023 for me.

Facts:
– The cat's name is "Professor Jones"
– He's the cat of a Reddit user, /u/Lavonicus
